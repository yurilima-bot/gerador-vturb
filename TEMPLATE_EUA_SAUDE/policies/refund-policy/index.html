REFUND POLICY

1. INTRODUCTION

At Our Company, we strive to ensure customer satisfaction while complying with U.S. consumer protection laws, including but not limited to:

  • The Federal Trade Commission Act (FTC Act)

  • The Magnuson-Moss Warranty Act

  • The California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) (for California residents)

  • The Uniform Commercial Code (UCC) (applicable to sale of goods)

This Refund Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds, exchanges, and returns are accepted. By purchasing from our website, you agree to this policy.

2. STANDARD REFUND POLICY (7-DAY RIGHT OF WITHDRAWAL)

Under federal consumer protection laws, customers are entitled to a 7-day refund period (also known as the "Right of Withdrawal") if the product is returned in its original, unused condition.

Eligibility for a refund within 7 days:

  • The product must be unused, unopened, and in its original packaging.

  • The original receipt or proof of purchase is required.

  • Refund requests must be initiated via email (support@ourcompany.com).

  • Shipping fees are non-refundable.

Customers are responsible for return shipping costs, except in cases of product defects or incorrect shipments.

3. 60-DAY SATISFACTION GUARANTEE

We offer an extended 60-day satisfaction guarantee under the following conditions:

  • Only one bottle may be opened.

  • The remaining bottles must be sealed and in original condition.

  • The product must be returned within 60 days from the delivery date.

  • This guarantee applies only to first-time purchases (subsequent orders are not covered).

  • Refunds will be processed once the returned product is received and inspected.

Refund Processing Time:

  • Refunds are issued within 7 business days upon receipt and inspection of the returned item.

  • Refunds will be credited back to the original payment method.

  • If payment was made via credit card, processing times depend on your bank or payment gateway.

4. NON-REFUNDABLE ITEMS & EXCEPTIONS

We do not accept returns or refunds for:

  • Used or partially consumed products beyond the one-open-bottle policy.

  • Products returned without the original receipt or order number.

  • Final sale items, promotional bundles, or discounted products.

  • Shipping fees, handling costs, and gift wrap charges.

5. RETURN PROCESS

To initiate a refund request, follow these steps:

  1. Contact our support team at suporte@wemegalife.com with your order number, full name, and reason for return.

  2. If eligible, our team will provide a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number and return instructions.

  3. Pack the product securely and include your RMA number inside the package.

  4. Ship the return to the address provided by our support team.

  5. Keep your tracking number as proof of return shipment.

  6. Upon receipt, we will inspect the item and process your refund accordingly.

6. CHARGEBACKS & FRAUD PREVENTION

We take fraud prevention seriously. Chargeback abuse (fraudulent disputes) will result in:

  • Permanent account suspension.

  • Reporting to credit bureaus and payment processors.

  • Legal action if necessary.

If you have an issue with your order, please contact us first before filing a dispute.

7. CONSUMER RIGHTS UNDER STATE LAWS

Certain U.S. states have additional consumer protection laws that may provide extended refund rights. California residents, for example, may have additional protections under the CCPA.

If you believe your refund rights under state law differ from this policy, please contact us at suporte@wemegalife.com.
